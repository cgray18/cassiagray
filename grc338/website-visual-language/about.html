About

Welcome to Sakana-suki, a culinary oasis where you can experience the art of sushi like never before. Nestled in the heart of the San Luis Obispo, our restaurant is the perfect destination for discerning foodies looking to indulge in the finest sushi creations made with the freshest ingredients. At Sakana-suki, we take great pride in creating beautiful and unique sushi dishes that are not only visually stunning but also bursting with flavor. Our skilled chefs are experts in the art of sushi making and use only the highest quality ingredients to ensure that every bite is a taste of perfection.

Our menu features a wide range of traditional and contemporary sushi dishes that are sure to delight even the most discerning palates. From our classic nigiri and sashimi to our innovative rolls and hand-crafted sushi art, each dish is a work of culinary art that is designed to showcase the unique flavors and textures of the ingredients. Our commitment to quality extends beyond our food to the dining experience itself. Our luxurious and intimate atmosphere is perfect for romantic dinners, business meetings, or special occasions. Our attentive staff is dedicated to providing impeccable service, and we strive to make every visit to Sakana-suki a memorable one.

We believe that sushi is not just food, but also an art form. Our chefs are passionate about creating beautiful and unique sushi dishes that are designed to delight the senses and leave a lasting impression. Whether you are a sushi aficionado or new to the world of sushi, Sakana-suki has something to offer everyone. We invite you to experience the artistry of Sakana-suki and taste the difference that fresh, high-quality ingredients can make. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more.
